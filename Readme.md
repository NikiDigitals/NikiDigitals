## Life Enterprise System (LES)

An enterprise-grade ERP architected around my life as the organisation. <br> 
Every life domain is treated as a business department with its own data model, processes, intelligence layer, and dedicated AI agent.

| Module | Department | Real ERP Equivalent |
|---|---|---|
| LES-FIN | Finance & Wealth Management | SAP FI / Oracle Financials |
| LES-EDU | Education & Knowledge Acquisition | LMS + Knowledge Management |
| LES-HLT | Health & Physical Performance | HR / Workforce Analytics |
| LES-PRJ | Projects, Goals & Execution | PPM / Project Systems |
| LES-REL | Relationships & Network | CRM |
| LES-OPS | Daily Operations & Routines | Operations Management |
| LES-KNW | Knowledge Base & Research | Enterprise Content Management |
| LES-INT | Intelligence & Analytics | BI / Advanced Analytics |
| LES-AGT | AI Agent Orchestration | Intelligent Automation |
| LES-SEC | Security, GRC & Compliance | GRC / Security Operations |

**Primary cloud:** Microsoft Azure  
**AI strategy:** Custom-built multi-agent system. Azure OpenAI Service as LLM infrastructure. Agent architecture, orchestration, and decision logic designed and built as part of LES.  
**Timeline:** 5 years to expert level. Ongoing development thereafter.
